EVENT HIRE 

As a performer, I am available for gigs at a variety of events types as a musician, actor and as an MC.Some of the events I commonly specialise in are: 

  • Weddings
  • Birthdays
  • Anniversities
  • Cafes
  • Bars
  • Festivals
  • Etc.

With my vast experience as a performer, I am able to adapt to the environment I am put in. I lug, set-up and pack-up my own equipment in a timely manner to ensure my costumers get the best from their money.                

When booking me as an artist, you can hire me in a variety of ways, such as:   

  • Solo Vocals
  • Small Ensemble Vocals
  • Full Band
  • Specialty songs dedicated to someone or something
  • Children film characters (princesses, faries, etc.)
  • Etc.
