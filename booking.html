How to Book 

There is nothing I love more than sharing music with the world, but unforunately I can't be in several places at once. For that reason, booking must occur at least one week prior to a local event, or at least two weeks for any out of area events that will result in more than two hours of travel.  This allows me time to prepare to make your event as memorable as possible.    

To ensure that I do not lose work as a result of cancellations, there is a booking fee of $50 that will be deducted from the total cost of the boking after the event. There is also a $100  fee in place for any cancellations with less than 24hrs notice. It is also required that for all private parties, one half of the payment is made before the performance.           

To book you must first check availabilities through my public calender and then contact me via my email, tarnni.foster@gmail.com for a swift reply, or through my socials which can be found in contacts.
